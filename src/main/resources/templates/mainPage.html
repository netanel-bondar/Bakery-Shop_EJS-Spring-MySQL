hadarim bakery is designed in a modern urban style and attracts young people, families, businessmen and tourists.
The bakery overlooks a view of a natural forest and the Jerusalem mountains.
On beautiful autumn and spring days, or on bright winter days, you can sit on the balcony and inhale the clear, wine-like pine air along with the aroma of the coffee and the smell of the chef's excellent dishes, which whets the appetite.
A casual urban atmosphere, unbuttoned and welcoming in the Jerusalem style, will welcome you whether you jump for a fragrant espresso with the morning newspaper or whether you arrive in full force for lunch or dinner.
